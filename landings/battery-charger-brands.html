Pain points for this segment

Supply-chain variants

Cell grade or BMS revision changes mid-season; documentation lags what is in the carton.

Markets vs SKUs

Same charger, different plug or bundle—scope of conformity evidence must stay explicit.

Instructions and warnings

Thermal runaway language, disposal icons, and multilingual manuals are easy to under-ship under margin pressure.

Channel questions

Marketplaces and carriers ask different questions than your design team—alignment breaks at the handoff.

Use it as an internal prep aid. Third parties may require their own forms and evidence—do not overstate what the snapshot represents.
