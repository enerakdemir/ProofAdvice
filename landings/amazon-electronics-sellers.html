Concrete pain points we hear from Amazon electronics sellers

Supplier PDF roulette

A CE “certificate” arrives that does not clearly map to your exact model, voltage, or firmware—yet listings go live under time pressure.

Listing vs technical file drift

Marketing updates range, accessories, or plugs faster than the technical file and test scope keep up.

EMC and safety together

Anything with a battery, mains lead, or radio module triggers multiple documentation threads—easy to under-scope until a case opens.

Unclear economic operator story

You sell across EU and UK but addresses, importer lines, and responsible-person expectations differ by market and channel policy.

Short FAQ

Does this guarantee my ASIN will pass Amazon compliance?

No. Amazon applies its own policies and checks. CertPath helps you prepare evidence and spot gaps earlier—it does not replace Amazon’s decisions.

Is this legal advice?

No. It is decision-support and triage. Use qualified counsel and conformity professionals where your risk or complexity warrants it.

How long does the intake take?

Most teams finish in a few minutes if product facts are at hand. Incomplete answers still produce a snapshot, but assumptions will be flagged.

What if we already have test reports?

Upload thinking still happens outside CertPath today—use the output to verify scope alignment between reports, listings, and packaging before you rely on them in surveillance.
