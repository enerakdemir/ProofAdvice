Playbooks by seller type

Your economic operator role changes which documents you must hold, which names and addresses appear on packaging, and how you respond to market surveillance. CertPath’s intake captures seller role so your snapshot aligns with these patterns—always verify against current law and your contracts.

Brand / manufacturer

Focus: Design history, test planning, change control, factory production control (where applicable), and accurate DoC. Marketplaces will tie listings to your brand identity—keep revision control tight between firmware, hardware, and packaging.

Importer (EU / UK concepts)

Focus: Verifying the foreign manufacturer’s file, supplementing traceability for your jurisdiction, and ensuring your importer details on the product are truthful. You may need to engage an AR or test lab if the manufacturer cannot support EU/UK requirements.

Reseller / distributor

Focus: Chain of custody for existing conformity, batch traceability, and ensuring you do not alter the product or claims in ways that invalidate supplier documentation. If you repackage or white-label, assume your obligations rise sharply.
