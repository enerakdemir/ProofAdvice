Checklist

Amazon technical product compliance intake checklist

Use this list to collect consistent facts about a SKU before you commission tests or legal review. It mirrors the kind of structured intake CertPath uses internally. It does not determine legal conformity by itself.

1. Product identity

  • Commercial name and model / variant identifiers.
  • Listing URL (Amazon or other) if live—or intended channel.
  • Target age group if the product is aimed at or attractive to children.

2. Sales channel & role

  • Marketplace vs own site vs wholesale.
  • Your role: brand owner, manufacturer, importer, distributor, or reseller.
  • Whether you control design, sourcing, packaging text, or only resale.

3. Technical risk signals

  • Mains powered, rechargeable, or battery-powered (including coin cells).
  • Wireless: Bluetooth, Wi‑Fi, proprietary RF, cellular.
  • Skin or prolonged body contact.
  • Heating, motors, lasers, or other energy hazards.

4. Markets & claims

  • Great Britain, Northern Ireland, EU member states (as applicable).
  • Marketing claims on packaging or listing (waterproof, medical-style language, child suitability, etc.).

5. Evidence already held

  • Supplier declarations, test summaries, risk assessments (if any).
  • Instructions and labelling artwork—even drafts.
