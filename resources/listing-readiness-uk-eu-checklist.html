Checklist

UK/EU listing readiness checklist

Marketplaces and regulators care about consistency between what you claim publicly and what your technical evidence supports. This checklist highlights common disconnects. It is not a guarantee that a listing will be approved or that a product is lawful to place on the market.

  • Title & bullets — Do performance claims (range, speed, capacity) match packaging and any test reports you can produce?
  • Images & icons — Are CE/UKCA marks shown only where truthful and aligned with your economic-operator setup?
  • Warnings & age labelling — For toys or child-attractive products, do warnings and imagery match the technical file and standards referenced?
  • Languages — Do instructions and safety text meet market expectations (UK vs EU member state)?
  • Traceability — Batch or serial identification, manufacturer identity, and contact for complaints—can you answer a surveillance request within hours?
  • Spares & firmware — If you promise updates or accessories, is that reflected in conformity planning?
