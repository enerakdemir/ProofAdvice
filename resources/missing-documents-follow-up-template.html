We are preparing conformity and marketplace documentation for [product name], model [xxx], which we intend to place on the market in [UK / EU / both] via [channel].

Please provide the following by [date], in PDF where possible, with clear revision dates and scope (which variants each document covers):

1) Declaration of Conformity (DoC) aligned to the current design revision.
2) Test reports or summaries covering electrical safety and EMC (or a short statement if not applicable and why).
3) Risk assessment / design documentation index sufficient for traceability.
4) Final label artwork and instruction manual for [languages].
5) Point of contact for post-market issues (PMS) if different from your sales desk.

Outstanding from your previous reply:
• [Gap 1]
• [Gap 2]

If any item cannot be supplied, please explain the commercial or technical constraint so we can assess next steps.

Kind regards,
[Name]
