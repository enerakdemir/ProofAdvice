What a CertPath compliance snapshot looks like

CertPath separates a deterministic rule engine layer (authoritative lists) from an optional AI explanation layer (plain language). This walkthrough helps you interpret outputs and decide when to move to Starter Pack or Expert Review (see support tiers).

1. Product & channel summary

Restates your intake: name, URL, channel, category, role, CE status, markets, and key risk answers. Use it to confirm you did not mis-click before trusting downstream lists.

2. Likely compliance domains & documents

Rule-derived themes such as electrical safety, radio, batteries, or product-specific documentation. These are triage hypotheses, not an exhaustive legal classification.

3. Gaps, risk flags, next steps

Structured prompts on missing evidence, elevated risk patterns, and pragmatic “what to do next” ordering. Pair with your own technical and legal judgement.

4. Optional AI explanation

When enabled, summarises the rule output in readable language and may reference public guidance pages for tone. It must not contradict the rule engine; if wording feels off, trust the structured lists.
