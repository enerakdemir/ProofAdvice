Playbook

Amazon listing risk review before launch

Use this playbook as a structured desk review after engineering and before marketing goes live. It complements—not replaces—Amazon policy checks, safety testing, and legal review of claims.

A. Claims discipline

List every superlative and performance metric on the detail page. For each, mark: “supported by test or spec”, “marketing only”, or “needs removal / softening”. Pay attention to medical or child-safety adjacent wording.

B. Visual consistency

Compare main image, infographic callouts, and A+ content against the physical product and packaging. Mismatched plugs, accessories, or region-specific marks are a frequent enforcement trigger.

C. Document cross-check

Spot-check that the SKU in test summaries matches voltage, radio modules, and firmware referenced on the page. If you offer bundles, confirm each component is covered or clearly excluded.

D. Post-launch monitoring

Assign an owner to watch returns reasons, Voice of the Customer, and competitor complaints for the first 60 days—feed findings back into conformity and listing updates.
