About CertPath

Practical UK/EU compliance triage for teams that ship physical products.

Who we help

CertPath is aimed at organisations that need a credible first pass on certificates, conformity evidence, and common risk themes before they invest in deeper consulting or testing.

  • SMEs & brands

    In-house teams launching or scaling SKUs into the UK and EU who need a shared snapshot of likely requirements and gaps.

  • Marketplace sellers

    Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, and similar sellers who must align product evidence with channel expectations and market surveillance reality.

  • Resellers & importers

    Economic operators clarifying how responsibilities may split between supplier and seller—and what to verify before placing products on the market.

  • Technical products

    Electronics, accessories, powered devices, and related goods where electrical safety, radio, batteries, or materials documentation often matter.

What we believe

  • Transparency builds trust. Users should see which parts of a result come from fixed rules versus optional AI wording.
  • Humans stay in the loop for high-stakes decisions. Software can accelerate triage; it should not replace professional judgement where law or safety requires it.
  • Market access is a team sport. Good outcomes combine engineering, supply chain, quality, and external specialists—not a single automated score.

What CertPath is not

CertPath is not a notified body, certification scheme operator, law firm, or marketplace. We do not issue EU-type examination certificates or UKCA “approvals.” We do not represent any government or platform.
